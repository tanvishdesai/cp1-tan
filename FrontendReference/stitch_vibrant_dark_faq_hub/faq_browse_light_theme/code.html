How does the revenue split work?

add remove

Our platform operates on a transparent 80/20 revenue split. Experts retain 80% of all earnings generated through consultations, webinars, and direct queries. The 20% platform fee covers payment processing, hosting, marketing, and semantic search infrastructure. Payouts are processed on the 1st and 15th of every month.

What is the required response time for queries?

expand_more expand_less

To maintain high quality standards, experts are expected to respond to direct queries within 48 hours. If you are unable to meet this SLA, you can activate "Vacation Mode" in your Profile settings to temporarily pause incoming requests.

How do I verify my credentials?

expand_more expand_less

Credential verification is handled securely through our partner portal. Navigate to Settings > Verification, and upload a valid government ID alongside your professional certifications. The review process typically takes 3-5 business days.
